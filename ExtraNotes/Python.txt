#Slicing
You can slice a string, a list, a numpy array,
For instance the expression s[::-1] where s is a string would mean:

1. s is your string or sequence.
2. The square brackets [] indicate slicing.
3. Inside the brackets, the :: specifies the start and end of the slice, and what comes after it is the step.
4. The first empty space before the first colon : means "start at the beginning of the string".
5. The second empty space after the first colon and before the second colon : means "all the way to the end of the string".
